TECHNOLOGY STACK

CV Interview Technology

Intelligent Document Processing

Advanced PDF parsing and text extraction using LangChain framework. Extracts structured information from your resume including skills, experience, and qualifications for personalized interview generation.

Vector Embeddings & FAISS

Converts your resume into semantic embeddings stored in FAISS (Facebook AI Similarity Search) vector database. Enables lightning-fast, context-aware question generation and retrieval based on your specific background.

Groq LLM Integration

Powered by Groq's ultra-fast Large Language Model (LLM) for intelligent question generation and answer evaluation. Provides real-time, human-like interview interactions with personalized feedback based on your profile.

Smart Content Chunking

Text splitting algorithms that break down resume content into optimal chunks for processing. Ensures every detail from your experience is indexed and can be referenced during interview question generation.

Real-Time Answer Evaluation

Combines vector similarity search with LLM reasoning to evaluate your responses. Retrieves relevant context from your resume, compares with your answer, and generates detailed performance insights instantly.

Persistent Progress Tracking

Django ORM with SQLite database stores every interview session, question, answer, and evaluation. Access complete interview history, performance trends, and improvement analytics across all your practice sessions.
